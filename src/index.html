About Home CARE

Did Covid time prevent you from receiving health services, and did you feel that you should be able to assist your parents' health remotely? We had the same experience with our seniors, therefore our team created Home CARE, a one-stop platform for our loved ones' health. Home CARE will respond with the same love to share with our seniors as we received from them. It's an easy to use platform with features that can assist you every step of the way!

Our Features?

Timely Updates & Reminders

We care about you and we don't want you to miss out on your vital health information, so we have Timely Updates to keep you on track!

One-on-One Messaging & Appointment Bookings

One-on-One chatting with your nurse to keep them up to speed on your medicine remotely, and you can make appointments with your doctor based on their schedule, so you don't have to worry about missing appointments; we'll always remind you ahead of time!!

Password-Encrypted Reports

Have work, can't collect your reports? Don't worry, we've got your back! We keep your reports private for you by encrypting them with a password that only you have access to!

Entertainment

Want to learn more about how to improve your health and prevent harmful behaviours? We've got you covered! We have Daily Reads for you where you may learn about everything from the greatest exercises and meditation to the healthiest foods to eat!
